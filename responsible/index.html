Responsible Gambling Policy

We want our players to have fun while playing on our platform. However, we understand that for some, gambling can become a problem. We are committed to helping our customers play responsibly and providing the tools necessary to stay in control.

Protecting Minors

Participation in gambling activities is strictly limited to individuals aged 18 and over. We perform rigorous age verification checks to prevent minors from accessing our platform. We encourage parents to use filtering software to prevent children from accessing gambling content.

Staying in Control

Gambling should be seen as a form of entertainment and not as a way to earn an income. To help you maintain control, we suggest the following:

Set a budget before you start and stick to it.

Don't gamble if you are feeling distressed or lonely.

Take frequent breaks.

Never chase your losses.

Responsible Gambling Tools

We offer a range of features to help you manage your activity:

Deposit and Loss Limits

You can set limits on the amount of money you can deposit or lose over a specific period (daily, weekly, or monthly). Once these limits are reached, you will not be able to exceed them until the period expires.

Time-Out and Self-Exclusion

If you need a break, you can use our Time-Out feature to suspend your account for a short period. For longer-term concerns, we offer a self-exclusion option that prevents you from accessing your account for a minimum of six months.

Recognizing a Problem

Signs that gambling may be becoming an issue include spending money intended for essentials, neglecting work or family, and lying about the amount of time or money spent gambling. If you recognize these signs in yourself or someone else, please seek help.

Professional Support

There are many organizations that provide free and confidential advice for anyone struggling with gambling habits. We encourage anyone who feels they are losing control to reach out to professional counseling services available in their local region.
